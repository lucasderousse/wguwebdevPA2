Welcome to Missouri

The state of Missouri, which was admitted to the Union on August 10, 1821, is home to many cities and walks of life. The state currently has a population of 6,160,281 as of the 2020 census. Its average income rate stands at $30,662, and a median household income of $57,290.

Did you know?

Missouri has it's own dialect of french, Missouri french (or Paw Paw French) is a variant of French spoken in the upper Mississippi region.
