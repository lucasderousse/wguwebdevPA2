Capital, ho!

Welcome to Jefferson City, the capital of the great state of Missouri. With a population of 43,228 at the 2020 census, this makes it the 15th most populous city in Missouri. While Jefferson City (informally known as Jeff City) was laid out in the 19th and the Missouri Territory was organized, it would not become the capital of the state until 1821, but incorporated as a city in 1825. It is currently the county seat of Cole County, located at the very north of the Ozark Plateau.It is an Urban city, with an Average income level of $31,781, roughly on par with the rest of the state.

Did you know?

  • The city was named after Thomas Jefferson.
  • The city is classified as having a humid continental climate
  • Jeff City was struck by an EF-3 tornado in 2019.
