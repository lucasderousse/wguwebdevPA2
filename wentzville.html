Crossroads of the Nation

Wentzville, Missouri is the fastest growing city in Missouri for two consecutive decades, with the population being 44,372 as of the last census.The city was founded in 1855, and resides in St. Charles County just next to St. Louis. It is a now mostly a suburban city, with an average household income of $45,274, about $15,000 higher than the state average.

Did you know?

Wentzville is named for Erasmus Livingston Wentz, who was the chief engineer of the Northern Missouri Railroad.
