Gateway to the West

Welcome to St. Louis, the (almost) capital of the great state of Missouri. With a population of 301,578 at the 2020 census, this makes it the 2nd most populous city in Missouri. It was founded on February 14, 1764 and incorporated in 1822. The wonderful city is located right on the border of illinois, with the directly neighboring city also being named St. Louis. It is an Urban city, with an Average income level of $29,892, just a bit below the rest of the state.

Did you know?

St. Louis has the largest metropolitan area in Missouri, and the 2nd largest in Illinois!
